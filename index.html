About me

Hi!

I am a pre-doctoral researcher at the University of Zurich, working on topics in labor economics and inequality. Before joining UZH, I completed my Master’s degree in Economics at the Vienna University of Economics and Business (WU), with stays at Université de Lausanne (UNIL) and the London School of Economics (LSE).

Research Interests

In my research I am interested in labor markets and income & wealth inequalities.

I want to understand the drivers of inequality, such as technological change and how people perceive these inequalities.

Working papers:

  • "The Effects of Minimum Wage Increases on Poverty and Food Hardship" (with Lehner, Parolin, Schmitt) IZA working paper
  • (Mis)perceptions of Inequality and their determinants" (with Franetovic, Löffler, Memmen, Moya, Schmitt)
