Research

In my research I am interested in labor markets and income & wealth inequalities.

I want to understand the drivers of inequality, such as technological change and how people perceive and are impacted by these inequalities.

Working papers:

  • "The Effects of Minimum Wage Increases on Poverty and Food Hardship" (with Lehner, Parolin, Schmitt) IZA working paper
  • (Mis)perceptions of Inequality and their determinants" (with Franetovic, Löffler, Memmen, Moya, Schmitt)
